About Viking

About

Viking is a Twitch streamer primarily focusing on the game "Rainbox Six:Siege" and "Rocket League". He has been a gamer almost all of his life and hasn't thought about actually making things serious until fairly recently. He started streaming back in his PS4 days where he used the very terrible program Sony had to stream from a PS4 instead of an Elgato device. During then, his streaming was not at all serious, he expected no viewers really. Then he sold his PS4 to venture into the PC space, a fairly new space for the 19 year old Viking! He was able to save up some money and ended up buying a PC at $350 which had included: an AMD FX processor, an R9 280x, and 8gb of ram. It was pretty much a budget friendly system, and all that mattered is that it played Rocket League! He then spent the next two years nose diving into the PC space and Tech and is now very knowledgable in both areas, primarily more into PCs. He wishes to make tech his career, and you lovevly people coming to check out him stream and giving him notoriety is amazing.
